Call for Participation

In order for your team to register to PIR-FIRE 2024:

  1. One member of the team should fill in this form with information about the team members, their emails and affiliations.
  2. The name and email of one member of the team that acts as the team representative must also be provided.
  3. The team representative must request to join the PIR-FIRE Google Group at https://groups.google.com/g/pir-fire-2024/. All official communications will be made through this Google Group. Other members may join too.

All requests should be made using the email addresses specified in the form. For questions and information you can contact us at pirfire.unimib@gmail.com.
